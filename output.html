Mars ReFab Hub — Output Ideas


Recast Kitchen Utensils
Durable spoons, cups, and trays made by melting and reshaping leftover aluminum frames and metal scraps from other modules.
Use: Everyday tools for Mars habitat kitchens

Inputs

1
Aluminum frames and metallic scraps (melted down)
2
Thermal furnace for metal smelting
3
3D molds or pressing tools for reshaping
4
Protective gear & cooling setup
Insulation Foam Panel

Inputs

1
Foam packaging (Zotek / Plastazote)
2
Textile scraps (covering)
3
Minimal adhesive / mechanical fasteners
Carbon-Based Filter
Air & water purification system made from mission carbon residues and paper waste
Use: Filtration

Inputs

1
Carbon residue generated from astronauts’ oxygen extraction experiments
2
Paper/Cardboard waste (used as biochar feedstock)
3
Minimal water for activation and washing
