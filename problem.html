Limited Resources on Mars

Transporting all materials from Earth is impractical, so every waste product inside the Mars habitat must be reused.

Need for a Closed-Loop Recycling System

A self-sustaining system is required to convert mission waste (metal, plastic, paper, etc.) into useful resources.

PROBLEM
STATEMENT

Variety of Waste Materials

Different waste types — metals, plastics, foams, textiles — need customized recycling methods suited to each material’s properties.

Sustainability and Efficiency

The challenge is to create valuable new products with minimal energy and equipment, maintaining environmental safety on Mars.
